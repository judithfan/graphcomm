Guess My Sketch!

In this experiment you will play a fun communication game with another player on MTurk! In each round both of you are going to be shown a set of objects like this:

You'll each see the same four objects but the order they are in will be different.

One of you will be assigned the Sketcher role and the other will be the Viewer . On each round, one of the four objects is the target, which is outlined in orange. Only the Sketcher can see which object is the target.

The goal of the Sketcher is to make a sketch of the target so that the Viewer can figure out which object it is. You'll both get a bonus of $0.03 for each correct response, so pay attention! If you are the Sketcher, please do your best to make a sketch of each target from the perspective that is shown in the picture. Please do NOT draw text labels, arrows, or any background for the object.

The goal is for the Viewer to identify the target based on what the Sketcher has drawn. If you are the Viewer, you will first need to wait until the Sketcher has finished their sketch before making your selection.

Once the Viewer clicks on the object they believe is the target, both players will be given feedback. So, the Sketcher will see what the Viewer clicked, and the Viewer will see the true target. Then you will both automatically advance to the next round of objects. There are a total of 32 rounds. After the final round you will fill out a quick 15 second survey and be on your way.

A few final notes: First, since you are playing with another player, you may see this screen before the game begins:

Just hold tight. Another player should join the game within the next few minutes. If you're in the waiting room 15 minutes without another player joining, please return the HIT and try again later!

Second, please be respectful of the worker you're playing with: do not send inappropriate material to your partner (e.g., words), and try to be as responsive as possible.

Finally, please DO NOT refresh the page at any point once you start the game (including the waiting page). If you refresh, you will not be able to rejoin the game you were in and you may not be able to submit the HIT.
Thank you!
